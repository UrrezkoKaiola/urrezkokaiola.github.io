Personality

Biolina is devious and sly, but ultimately a kind person.

Story

Biolina, as The Violinist, was one of the five original members of the Band, along with Sintizadorea, Baxua, Ilargia, and Sopranoa.

She is one of the four members of The Band when they are encountered in Chapter Seven, acting as an obstacle in the Security Challenge.

Abilities

Baxua has violin-based abilities, which allow both healing and buffing her fellow band members.

Other Band Members

She seems to get along with the other Band members.
