Personality

Kara is stoic, calm, and collected. She very rarely shows any emotions besides disinterest, apathy, and mild annoyance.

Story

Kara appears in Chapter Two to alert the player that The Mansion is not supposed to be in Chapter Two.

Kara can be found inside the Code Splice of the Hotel Kaiola in Chapter Three, and she can be fought.

Kara is an optional boss fight in Chapter Four.

Kara fights the player if they chose to speak to Iklusea in Chapter Five.

She also fights the party if they chose to complete Iklusea's questline in Chapter Seven.

Abilities

Kara has significant control over the fabric of the Urrezko Kaiola world itself. Anything and everything inside it is susceptible to her ability. The only limit to this ability is her focus.

Kara has the ability to de-sync Avatars from their players.

Kara is completely immune to seduction attacks, because she is asexual.

Relationships

Abby

Abby loathes Kara, and will do anything to annoy or undermine her in order to achieve her goal of leaving Urrezko Kaiola.

Lance

Lance, like Abby, intensely dislikes Kara for the reason that she will not let characters leave the Urrezko Kaiola world.

Iruzurra

Iruzurra dislikes Kara due to her interference in the economy. At one point, she attacks him for running a toll scam.

Simulacra-1337

Simulacra-1337 also intensely dislikes Kara, and likes trolling her. He especially likes calling her a fairy (she's an angel) to get under her skin.

Iklusea

Kara and Iklusea are sisters, and absolutely hate each other for reasons that are not clear. Iklusea's prescence is one of the rare things that can make Kara actually angry.
