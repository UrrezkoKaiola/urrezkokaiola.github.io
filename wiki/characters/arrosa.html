Personality

Arrosa is a gregarious and ditzy character. He is known for being lovesick and flirtatious.

Story

Arrosa first appears chasing after Erretzen in the first area of Chapter Eight and introduces himself to the players after Erretzen runs away.

Throughout the chapter, he can be befriended by the player, or not.

At the end of the chapter, if he is befriended, he will attempt to sacrifice himself to save the player from Oihaneko Erregea. In most cases, he is stopped by another character, but if he is not stopped, he dies to little avail.

Abilities

Arrosa has access to Metronome and Grab Bag. This is because of the randomness factor of his Unique Ability, which he has forgotten.

Relationships

Erretzen

He has an unreciprocated crush on Erretzen, which annoys her.

Oihaneko Erregea

He was initially loyal to the Jungle King, but after witnessing his actions in the finale of the chapter chose to turn against him.
