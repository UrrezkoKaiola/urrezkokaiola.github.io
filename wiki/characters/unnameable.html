Personality

The Catboy Whose Name Leaves No Lips is kind and affable, except when dealing with Izoztutako (see Relationships).

Story

The Pinket is first seen following after a fight with Izoztutako in Chapter Six. He Whose Name is Infohazardous then fights the players.

The Nameless One (okay, now you're just pushing it) is a recruitable party member in Chapter Six, and can help you fight.

The Musical Catboy is recruitable in Chapter Seven, although only after collecting the six pieces of the Flute of Secrets.

Abilities

As you may have guessed, The Snarky Royal Attendant's Unique Ability prevents his name from being spoken aloud or in text. This even extends to the interface of the game itself. He requires a conscious effort to stop the ability from working, and so doesn't ever turn it off. Iklusea is capable of bypassing this ability for unknown reasons.

Pitchromantic Kitten along with Izoztutako, has access to the skill Melts My Heart, which weaponizes their dysfunctional romantic relationship (see Relationships) to heal him.

The Flute of Secrets allows Magenta Kitten to access music skills.

Relationships

Izoztutako

Izoztutako and He Of The Red Doors are romantically involved. Due to their clashing personalities, though, this almost always creates bickering-flirting shouting matches between them. He has an affectionate nickname for her, "Ice Bitch". Only he is allowed to call her that, at least according to her.

Euritsu

Rose-Haired Kitty and I are siblings. We get along just fine, but his ability does cause me problems. See the pink text.

Trivia

  • One song title in Chapter Six, "Hidden Caves of Kismesis", is a refererence to Izoztutako and That Catboy's relationship. It references the webcomic Homestuck, 'kismesis' being an in-universe concept of a relationship based on mutual and romantic hatred.
  • Mysterious Magical Cat is referred to as a Nekomata by his Class Title.
