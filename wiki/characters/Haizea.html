Personality

Haizea is normally fairly affable, but when annoyed, which is often, she becomes something of a bitch.

Story

Haizea was intended to be the primary antagonist of Chapter Four (originally Three), but was kicked out of that position by Simulacra-1337 and his minions Basati, Zelatatu, and Tximista. Subsequently, Haizea became a fairly minor character in the grand scheme of things.

Haizea returns in Chapter Six, now helping out Izoztutako.

Abilities

Haizea's Unique Ability allows her unparalleled control over the wind. Some unique applications of this include flight and forcing fight to end early by blowing away one of the combatants.

Haizea also has a golden Super Form, known as Super Haizea. This ability is apparently activated by yelling for a really long time.

Relationships

Simulacra-1337

Haizea absolutely hates Ross Bot, and he hates her in turn.

Tximista and Izoztutako

Izoztutako, Haizea, and Tximista are friends, and the latter two helped Izoztutako fight the Solvers.

Iruzurra

Iruzurra dislikes Haizea because of how she ran the Sky Realm.

Trivia

  • Haizea's Super Form is based off of similar transformations in Dragon Ball Z and Sonic the Hedgehog, and a common meme about transformations in the former series being activated by yelling.
  • Despite being a queen, Haizea only had one subject: Iruzurra. He soon left.
