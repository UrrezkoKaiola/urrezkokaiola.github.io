Personality

Izoztutako is an archetypical himedere; ordering people (even those who aren't her subjects) around, and expecting to be treated like a queen (which, to be fair to her, she is).

Story

Izoztutako appeared briefly in Chapter Three in the Hotel Kaiola. The Avatar fights her to gain a key. In Chapter Four, Izoztutako is incarcerated by Simulacra-1337 for unknown reasons. The Avatar can attempt to free Izoztutako, at which point she freezes them for the effort.

Izoztutako is the major antagonist of Chapter Six, being the queen of the Ice Realm it is set in.

Abilities

While all Ice Realm residents have some sort of ice powers, Izoztutako's Unique Ability gives her unparalleled control over the element.

Izoztutako, along with a certain catboy, has access to the skill Melts My Heart, which weaponizes their dysfunctional romantic relationship (see Relationships) to heal her.

Relationships

Pink-Haired Catboy

Izoztutako and The Catboy Who Likes to Hide In The Spaces Between are romantically involved. Due to their clashing personalities, though, this almost always creates bickering-flirting shouting matches between them. Izoztutako has an affectionate(?) nickname for him, "Gutter Tabby".

Euritsu

Izoztutako and I are good friends, but I don't have much more tolerance for her antics than others.

Haizea and Tximista

Izoztutako, Haizea, and Tximista are friends, and the latter two helped Izoztutako fight the Solvers.

Trivia

  • One song title in Chapter Six, "Hidden Caves of Kismesis", is a refererence to Izoztutako and the catboy's relationship. It references the webcomic Homestuck, 'kismesis' being an in-universe concept of a relationship based on mutual and romantic hatred.
  • Izoztutako is referred to as a Yuki-onna in one song title.
