Personality

Goizane was cheerful, but she was also a coward, scared of almost anything.

Story

In Chapter One, she stood in Urdina and cheerfully greeted the Avatar. In Chapter Two, she stood in the same place, this time talking about how Lehiakide scared her. In Chapter Three, she was found dead in a room of the Hotel Kaiola. It is unknown how this happened, besides that Abusari was involved.

Relationships

Avatars

Each of the Avatars mourn Goizane when they see her dead body.

Lehiakide

Goizane was scared of Lehiakide for reasons not known.

Abusari

Goizane was found dead in the Hotel Kaiola, which Abusari runs. It is unknown if Abusari killed her, but it is likely.
