Iklusea's Unique Ability is remembering the loops. As I can't remember the Loops, I can only speculate on what this ability's limits and uses are.

Iklusea has a number of skills related to seduction and sexuality.

Iklusea can, for some reason, become stronger by stripping (to her underwear or naked). This may be because her opponents are distracted, or it just increases her skills' potency.
