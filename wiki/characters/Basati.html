Personality

Basati was very laconic, not prone to speaking. He seemed to have a great enjoyment for fighting, and enjoyed serving Simulacra-1337 because it allowed him to do so.

Story

Basati, along with Zelatatu and Tximista, were unused characters banished to the Infinite Field. When Abusari changed the Chapter order by making The Hotel Kaiola its own chapter, the barrier between the Infinite Field and the rest of Urrezko Kaiola thinned enough that Simulacra-1337's ability could take himself, along with Basati, Zelatatu, and Tximista, into the game proper.

Basati is a minor antagonist under Ross Bot in Chapter Four, along with Haizea, Tximista, and Zelatatu.

Basati, along with Zelatatu, was killed by Tximista with lightning.

Abilities

Basati had incredible physical power. This is his only known ability.

Simulacra-1337

Basati served Ross Bot, and seemed to enjoy it.

Tximista

Tximista killed Basati. Why she did this is not known.
