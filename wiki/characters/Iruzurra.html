Personality

Iruzurra is greedy, and will do anything to obtain money. He will go to almost any end to do this.

Story

In Chapter Four, Iruzurra introduces himself when the player enters the Sky Realm.

Iruzurra continues acting as a shopkeeper further on in Chapter Five and Six.

At one point in chapter Six, Iruzurra opens a 'toll water lane'. If the player is charged four times to go through this lane, Kara appears and attacks Iruzurra, disabling the toll water lane.

Abilities

Iruzurra seems to be able to be in multiple places at once, but it is not known whether this is what he's actually doing or if he just quickly teleports to follow the player.

Iruzurra has a recurring option called 'Surprise Me' which can cause any number of teleportation or interface-screwing effects to the player.

Relationships

Kara

Iruzurra dislikes Kara due to her interference in the economy. At one point, she attacks him for running a toll scam.

Iruzurra

Iruzurra dislikes Haizea because of how she ran the Sky Realm.
