Personality

Amorratu is tired of his job, and as his name suggests, often annoyed.

Story

Amorratu is the so-called owner of Jatetxe Ona Pizzeria, but according to him, he's just the cashier and The Band holds the real power.

Relationships

The Band

While allegedly the owner, Amorratu is in practice a subordinate to the members of The Band.

Iruzurra

Amorratu does not like Iruzurra because he sells his pizza at a markup and uses mafia-like tactics to maintain this arrangement.
