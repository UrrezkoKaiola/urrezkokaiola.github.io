Story

Urrezko Kaiola Bot is a Simulacra of the Creator of Urrezko Kaiola.

Simulacra-7779 has not yet appeared, and probably should not ever appear.

Abilities

Simulacra-7779 is responsible for fixing Urrezko Kaiola when characters cause it to break. For example, he placed items that undo Hyper Shield when Abusari gained access to the ability.

He is also responsible for the game's narration. When he is speaking directly to the player rather than simply narrating, he speaks in grey text.
