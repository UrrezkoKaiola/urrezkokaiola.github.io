Personality

Amets is something of a dork. He's considerably more verbose than the other avatars, not laconic like Nikole or Aintzane and not hammy like Martzel.

Story

Amets is one of four Avatars who can act as your player character throughout the chapters.

Players who choose Nikole or Aintzane will fight Amets, along with Martzel, in Chapter Five.

Abilities

Amets' signature skill is Fireball.

Relationships

Solvers

Amets is more proactive about warning the Solvers about danger than any other Avatar.
