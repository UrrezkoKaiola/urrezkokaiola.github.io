Personality

Much of Hirugarren's personality is unknown, and it is unlikely we'll ever get to know it, because she's dead.

Story

Lehen, Bigarren, and Hirugarren 'the catsketeers' occupy the South Ward of Urrezko City. After their boss battle, they retreat, only to be killed by Ilargia in seconds.

Abilities

Hirugarren is a competent fighter, but not incredibly strong.
