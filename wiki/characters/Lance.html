Personality

Lance is affable, but he's a bit of a doormat, especially when Abby is concerned.

Story

Outside the game, Lance helps out the players. Inside, he is a recurring boss, having fought the players once every chapter (with the exception of Chapter Zero, and a botched fight in Chapter Four).

For unknown reasons, Lance became a member of The Band. He became the Bassist, replacing a previous Bassist (female).

Abilities

The full extent of Lance's abilities is not known, because he has not yet fought seriously.

Lance cannot magically be put to sleep.

As The Bassist, Lance has a variety of sound-based attacks.

Relationships

Abby

Lance is Abby's second-in-command and trusted confidant, and they work together to achieve their goal of leaving Urrezko Kaiola. It is unknown if their relationship is platonic or romantic.

Kara

Lance, like Abby, intensely dislikes Kara for the reason that she will not let characters leave the Urrezko Kaiola world.

Trivia
