Personality

Jeremy Bot, like most Simulacra, is sarcastic and a bit of an asshole.

Story

Simulacra-2187, along with Simulacra-6561 and Simulacra-0729, challenge Lehiakide to a battle after they unlock their Unique Ability using the Internet Terminal.

Abilities

Jeremy Bot's Unique Ability allows him to speak in colored text. It's worth noting other characters have been seen to use colored text, but only in one color (besides white): dark grey. Jeremy Bot can use almost any color he wants, but prefers dark red, gold, dark grey, and black.
