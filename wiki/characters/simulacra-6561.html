Carmello Bot is incredibly serious.

Story

Simulacra-6561, along with Simulacra-2187 and Simulacra-0729, challenge Lehiakide to a battle after they unlock their Unique Ability using the Internet Terminal.

Abilities

Carmello Bot's Unique Ability allows him to copy the fighting style (read: skills and powers) of any opponent he has seen at least once. The limitation to this ability is that he can only use one copied fighting style at once. In-game, this move is called 'Trace'.
