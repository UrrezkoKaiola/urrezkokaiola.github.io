Personality

Zelatatu held (his own) intelligence in high esteem. He constantly bragged about his high IQ, despite game characters not having IQ as humans understand it.

Story

Zelatatu, along with Basati and Tximista, were unused characters banished to the Infinite Field. When Abusari changed the Chapter order by making The Hotel Kaiola its own chapter, the barrier between the Infinite Field and the rest of Urrezko Kaiola thinned enough that Simulacra-1337's ability could take himself, along with Basati, Zelatatu, and Tximista, into the game proper.

Zelatatu is a minor antagonist under Ross Bot in Chapter Four, along with Haizea, Tximista, and Basati.

Zelatatu, along with Basati, was killed by Tximista with lightning.

Abilities

Zelatatu's Unique Ability allowed him to create puzzles, including a Quiz, a puzzle with nine lights, and a puzzle involving moving statues.

Relationships

Solvers & Avatars

Zelatatu dislikes the Solvers, at one point threatening to delete their faces.

Simulacra-1337

Zelatatu served Ross Bot, and had no complaints about doing so.

Tximista

Tximista killed Zelatatu. Why she did this is not known.
