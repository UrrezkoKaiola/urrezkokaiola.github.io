Personality

Biderkatu is a system with four (known) alters. The most common are the blue-eyed 'scholar' personality and the green-eyed 'scaredy cat' personality. There is also the yellow-eyed personality who talks haltingly and the red-eyed 'angry' personality.

Story

Biderkatu stays in the Jungle Hub for most of the chapter. It's likely he had a larger role in the original Chapter Eight before Ashton Bot took over.
