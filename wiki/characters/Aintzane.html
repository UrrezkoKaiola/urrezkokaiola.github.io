Aintzane is stoic, but not as much as Nikole.

Story

Aintzane is one of four Avatars who can act as your player character throughout the chapters.

Players who choose Amets or Martzel will fight Aintzane, along with Nikole, in Chapter Five.

Abilities

Aintzane's signature skills are Fury Attacks and Disabling Strike.
