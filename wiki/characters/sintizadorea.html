Personality

Sintizadorea is a quiet, serious, and reserved individual.

Story

Sintizadorea, as The Keyboardist, was one of the five original members of the Band, along with Bioina, Baxua, Ilargia, and Sopranoa.

She is one of the four members of The Band when they are encountered in Chapter Seven, acting as an obstacle in the Security Challenge.

Abilities

Sintizadorea has keyboard-based abilities, which include debuffs to the enemy and buffs to his fellow band members.
