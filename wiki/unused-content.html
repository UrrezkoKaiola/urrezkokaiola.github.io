Unused Content

What is a Unused Content?

Many games have unused content. However, due to the ever-changing and fourth-wall-less nature of Urrezko Kaiola, unused content can nonetheless be accessed through meddling by either the Solvers or characters in the game.

Unused Characters

Characters in this category include: Basati, Zelatatu, and Tximista.

Unused Items

Unused items include the Universal Communicator "100010001000", Shield Breakers, and Cigarettes "ITEM_NAME_CIGARETTES".
