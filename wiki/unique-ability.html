Unique Ability

What is a Unique Ability?

All Game Characters and Simulacra that inhabit the Urrezko Kaiola world have a Unique Ability or the potential to unlock one.

Unique Abilities are related either to the character's role in the story, their personality, or both.

Types of Unique Abilities

There are two major subtypes of Unique Ability; Elemental and Information. Elemental abilities (e.g. Tximista, Haizea, or Izoztutako) give the character greater control over their element than any other. Information abilities are exactly what they sound like: related to the discovery, preservation, or destruction of information (e.g. Abusari, The Strange Catboy Whose Name May Not Be Spoken, and me).

Unique Abilities that aren't?

There is one exception to the 'Unique' part of Unique Abilities: The Avatars. They all share the same unique ability, that is, being an Avatar.

In the case of Elemental Unique Abilities, while others may be able to use that element, the Unique Ability allows a level of control over it greater than any other character. For example, while Martzel can use Shock and Awe and Lehiakide can use Thunderbolt, only Tximista can deal actual millions of damage with electricity. Other characters have Wind-based powers, but only Haizea can end an ongoing battle by sweeping away one of the combatants with wind, and so on.
